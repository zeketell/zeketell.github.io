Our Story

Sitting beneath the Sanitation Department of New York's crystalline solid, Salt Shed Coffee is inspired by the Art Commission Design Award-winning structure used to store the city's road salt, located in downtonwn Manhattan at Spring Street and the West Side Highway.

What We Do

We brew coffee made by hard working producers around the world to serve to our downtown customers.
